The David's Sling

Thy servant kept his father's sheep, and there came a lion… and I went out after him, and smote him… Thy servant slew both the lion and the bear.

1 Samuel 17:34–36 · King James Version

Before he was a king, before the giant, David was a shepherd. When lions came for his flock, he met them with a sling and a steady hand. Goliath is still to come — but first, the lions.

How to play

  • Hold the mouse to draw the sling — the longer you hold, the harder the throw.
  • Aim with your cursor and release to hurl the stone.
  • Strike each lion before it reaches you. The sling reloads on its own.
  • Every lion that reaches you wounds David. Endure — each level grows fiercer.
